These are the rules

Objective

The objective of the game is to be the first to sink all the ships of the enemy player.

Rules

Each Player must set their ships in the beginning. Players always take turns, even after one has been hit. Players cannot move their ships while playing. Ships should always hav at least one free tile between them.

Beginning

The board player should be the first to set their ships. The web player has to press set twice on the last piece to send the positions.
